Mi Best Friend

Welcome to my little corner of the internet where I will share all the adventures with my best friend!

Friendship doubles joys and divides anxieties in half.

I have many friends, but only a few with whom I share a deep connection.

One of my closest friends is someone I met at university during personalized tutoring sessions.

About our friendship

I was her tutor, and she was a new student at the university. At first, our conversations were focused on her studies, but soon we discovered we had a lot in common.

During one of our tutoring sessions, we realized we both share a love for video games and a passion for teaching others. She teaches English to children, while I teach programming to kids. Our shared interest in gaming brought us even closer—we both enjoy playing titles like GTA V, Genshin Impact, and Mobile Legends together.

Our conversations often dive deep into topics we’re both passionate about. We love discussing emerging technologies and how quickly they’re advancing. For example, we recently talked about quantum computers and how they can perform complex calculations in minutes that would take a normal computer years to complete. Of course, we also chat about video games, discussing new events, strategies, and how to participate in different in-game activities.

We enjoy spending time together doing various activities. We often team up to play online games, attend university classes together, go on trips, and study side by side. It’s great to have a friend who shares so many of my interests and brings so much joy and inspiration to my life.
